Beta Testing the ReadBar app

Thank you for testing version 0.0.4 of ReadBar.

To install it on your iOS device please:

  1. Ensure you are viewing this page from the iOS device whose UDID you have supplied.
  2. Click on this link download ReadBar
  3. Tap 'Install' when prompted to do so by your device.
  4. Return to your device's homepage ... and wait a few moments while the app finishes downloading and installing.
  5. Open the app by tapping it's icon on your device

Please note that this app is still in development and that you are installing it at your own risk. We accept no liability whatsoever for any real or perceived damage caused by the app or your use of it.

If you have any concerns please get in touch before proceeding with the installation.
